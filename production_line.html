Production line

The Production Line is the set of methods and tools that make possible to develop custom software to our customers on time and with the expected quality. It should cover all the phases involved on the application development cycle from requirements to testing and hand-off to the client.

This toolset is decided by experts and should be attractive and easy for the developers and engagements to use. Therefore their deployment should be automated and provi-sioned within few hours from the moment a project starts.

The idea is to have this group of tools tightly integrated and deploy them as a "product" to be run on several levels:

  • As a shared service for the projects with general requirements.
  • At a BU level service for projects with regulation restrictions that disallow for the engagement assets to leave the country.
  • At an engagement level for projects with very specific requirements.
